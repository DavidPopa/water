# Pareto Analysis (80/20 Rule)

Identify the 20% of causes/actions that produce 80% of results for: **$ARGUMENTS**

## Process

1. **List all factors**: Enumerate every contributing factor, option, or action related to the problem.

2. **Estimate impact**: For each factor, estimate its relative contribution to the overall outcome (use percentages or high/medium/low).

3. **Rank by impact**: Sort factors from highest to lowest contribution.

4. **Draw the Pareto line**: Identify the ~20% of factors that account for ~80% of impact. These are the **vital few**.

5. **Separate vital few from trivial many**:

| Priority | Factor | Impact | Category |
|----------|--------|--------|----------|
| 1 | ... | ...% | VITAL FEW |
| 2 | ... | ...% | VITAL FEW |
| ... | ... | ...% | Trivial many |

6. **Actionable recommendation**: Focus resources exclusively on the vital few. For each vital factor, define one concrete next action.

## Output

- A ranked table of all factors with impact estimates
- Clear separation between vital few (top 20%) and trivial many (bottom 80%)
- Specific recommendation: what to focus on and what to deliberately ignore or deprioritize
