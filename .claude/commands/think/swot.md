# SWOT Analysis

Analyze **$ARGUMENTS** across four strategic dimensions.

**Opportunities** (external, positive — what can we capitalize on?)
- ...

**Threats** (external, negative — what could hurt us?)
- ...
- ...

## Strategic Combinations

Cross-reference the quadrants to generate strategy:

| Strategy | Combination | Action |
|----------|------------|--------|
| **Leverage** | Strength + Opportunity | Use strengths to capture opportunities |
| **Defend** | Strength + Threat | Use strengths to neutralize threats |
| **Improve** | Weakness + Opportunity | Fix weaknesses to unlock opportunities |
| **Mitigate** | Weakness + Threat | Address weaknesses before threats exploit them |

## Output

- Completed SWOT grid with specific, concrete items (not vague generalities)
- Top 3 strategic recommendations derived from the combinations above
- Single most critical action item to address first
