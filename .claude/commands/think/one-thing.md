# The ONE Thing

Find the single most important action for: **$ARGUMENTS**

Ask: "What is the ONE thing I can do, such that by doing it, everything else becomes easier or unnecessary?"

## Process

1. **List all possible actions**: What could you work on? Brainstorm without filtering.

2. **Apply the focusing question to each**:

| Action | Makes other things easier? | Makes other things unnecessary? | Leverage Score |
|--------|---------------------------|--------------------------------|---------------|
| ... | Yes/No (which ones?) | Yes/No (which ones?) | High/Med/Low |

3. **Identify dominoes**: Which action, if completed, causes other tasks to fall automatically? This is a domino — it creates a chain reaction.

4. **Eliminate distractions**: Cross out everything that is NOT the ONE thing. These are not bad tasks — they are just not THE task right now.

5. **Define it concretely**: The ONE thing must be:
   - Specific (not vague)
   - Actionable (you can start now)
   - Time-bound (when will it be done?)
   - Measurable (how do you know it worked?)

## Output

- The ONE thing, stated clearly and concretely
- Why it is the highest-leverage action (what it unlocks)
- What to deliberately ignore until the ONE thing is done
- The immediate next step to begin
